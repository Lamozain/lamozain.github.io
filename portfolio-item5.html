VOCATIONAL TRAINING Award Ribbon

BettyNwa Youth Foundation School Training

The BettyNwa Youth Foundation is a Not For Profit Organization present in the 36 states of the federation. The Kaduna Branch launched an in-school free training session for selected schools in Kaduna Metropolis.

In this school, we trained the pupils on making award ribbons. Over 45 Senior Secondary School students participated. The skills deriveable from the trainig are transferable to academic and non academic life. It takes patience, planning, organisation, collaboration, cooperation, and confidence to be able to come up with such a beautiful ribbon.

The students were so happy. They requested that we come again. We believe that the students will be better motivated to learn, have more confidence in themselves, and increase their productivity level both in school and at home.
