SDGsWLL Family Activation (2019)

Mother||Children

It is not enough to educate the children about the Sustainable Development Goals. They are from families.If we want a lasting impact, we must involve the homes from which they come.

World Largest Lessons, dedicated a whole week to educating families, communities, trade associations, etc on Sustainable Development Goals. As a volunteer, I held a discussion session with some children and families.

As an SDGs ambassador, I strongly believe that the goals are attainable if all hands are on deck.
