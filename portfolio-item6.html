BettyNwa Youth Foundation Summer Training

The mission of the Bettynwa Youth Foundation is not limited to youths in school alone. It includes all youths that are ready to be empowered and thereafter contribute their quota to the development of the nation.

Therefore, a summer training was organized for youths. Participants were trained in several skills ranging from shoe making, catering, computing to rug making. The training lasted for two solid weeks.

At the end of the training, participants were awarded certificates after showcasing what they made from the skills learnt through the training. We also celebrated ourselves. BettyNwa is one organization that cannot be tired of giving to the populace.
