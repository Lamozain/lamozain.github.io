SELF MOTIVATIONthrough texts

Artistic Virtue Cards

Humans live with other humans because there is joy in living together. Apart from helping one another physically, we also help to motivate ourselves. While some people believe that motivation is intrinsic, a majority subscribe to the time-tested fact that we can be motivated by others either through words or their actions.

The idea of designing Artistic Virtue Cards (AVC) hit me when I was describing one of my friends a few years ago. She was happy to hear the words I used to qualify her and was always trying to live towards displaying such noble virtues. Who wouldn't love to be referred to as being 'friendly, not easily angered, caring, and beautiful'?

So, I started designing virtue cards for my friends. They love it and I believe you will too. Just hit me up on any of my social media accounts and we are good to go.
