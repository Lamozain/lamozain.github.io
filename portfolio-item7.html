FUTURE BUILDINGCareer Talk

Want to become a Nurse?

Your future depends on your work. It takes great commitment and determination to acheive goals. You own the eqiupment for buiding your future. People can only guide or motivate you, but you are the master builder. Everyone has a great and bright future and the future begins today or better put now because the little things you do today gather up to become the future bundle. It is common knowledge that a house can not be built in a day or a week it takes a number of days to reach the finish line. These are also true for young adults and kids. Their future begin from the present.

So, we invited a nurse to talk to our pupils about what it takes to be a nurse. But why nurse? We settled on nursing because after interveiwing our pupils majority said they want to become a nurse. It was therefore necessary to satisfy the majority first. For over 45 minutes the nurse touched on the work of a nurse, the ethics of nursing, what it takes to be a nurse, and the benefits accruable to a nurse.

Guess what? All participants wanted to become a nurse. It was a great learning session for both learners and teachers. Watch out for my kids!
