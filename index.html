Hi, I am Amos Omofaiye

Educator||Data Analyst||Farmer

What I do

Design + Development

Designs are artistic but are also therapeutic and functional. Growing businesses require one form of design at one stage or the other of their life. I offer qualitative, competitive and affordable services in design and development. I design portfolio and business websites, instructional materials, educational and agricultural applications. I also develop innovative solutions to modern business, farming and educational challenges.

Research + Analysis

Research is the foudation of all knowledge. The research process is one charged with transforming data to information. My expertize cuts across research planning, research instrument design, data collection (enumeration) and data analysis. I particularly enjoy data gathering through digital means. My Data Analysis works have taken me through the coast of statistical packages and programming languages like SPSS, STATA, NCSS, MiniTab, Python, R, SQL, Postgresql, Microsoft Access among others. Data do speak. It takes expertize to hear them. I hear and understand them.

Education + Training

Quality education is an integral part of any society. My teaching work cuts across the impartation of 21st century skills in pre-basic, basic and post-basic students (K-12). I use such methods and paradigms as student leadership, project-based learning, game-based learning, design thinking, phonics, and coding to teach Literacy, Numeracy, and Science whilst also encouraging learners to actively participate in making decisions that impact on their community. Moreover, I train small-scale farmers on poultry, snail, fish, and vegetable production.

My Work

Who I am

Researcher, Developer, Educator, & Analyst based in Nigeria

Amos is a quintessential Teacher-Leader, Agriculturist (Researcher), Data Analyst, and Front-end web developer. As an educator, I call myself a Juvenile Mind Therapist as I work to orient my learners towards the attainment of their life goals. I love volunteering for causes that advance educational and youth development with particular focus on STEAM, TVET, and eradication of extreme poverty.

I have published online articles on poultry, snail and fish production at the small-scale level. My work also involves delivery of agricultural seminars on livestock production and publishing of agricultural books. Moreover, I work with young adults to train them in basic skills and ethics. This has led me to volunteering with various organizations that work with children and young adults. At present, I run an initiative called School Arme which is concerned with training students, young adults and teachers in coding, game-based, project-based, and design-based learning so as to enhance their learning, practice, and life. I derive pleasure in teaching and volunteering especially when such endeavours have the potential of causing a positive massive change in the life of the participants and their immediate community. Amos Omofaiye holds a Master of Science degree from University of Ilorin.
